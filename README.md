## Business Problem
Understanding how global billionaire wealth is distributed across industries, countries, and demographics requires clear, high-level indicators. Without structured KPIs and visual summaries, it is difficult to interpret wealth concentration patterns and demographic trends effectively.

## Business Objective
To analyse global billionaire data and present key summary KPIs alongside visual breakdowns that support insight-driven exploration of wealth distribution patterns.

## Project Overview
This project combines data analysis and business analysis to present a structured overview of billionaire statistics using an Excel-based dashboard. Core KPIs provide immediate context, while charts and filters enable deeper analysis across industries, countries, gender, and sources of wealth.

## My Role
- Data Analysis: data cleaning, aggregation, KPI calculation, and dashboard creation using Excel  
- Business Analysis: defining business questions, framing insights, and translating analytical findings into business implications  

## Dataset
The dataset contains global billionaire information, including:
- Rank
- Net worth
- Country
- Industry
- Source of wealth
- Gender and age

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
  - Pivot Tables  
  - Charts & Visualisations  
  - Filters & Slicers  

## Core KPIs
- Rank
- Total Net Worth
- Average Net Worth
- Average Age

## Key Business Questions Answered
- How concentrated is global billionaire wealth?
- Which industries and countries account for the highest net worth?
- What demographic patterns exist among global billionaires?
- How does wealth distribution vary by source and sector?

## Key Insights
- A small number of individuals and countries account for a disproportionately large share of total net worth.
- Technology, Fashion & Retail, and Finance-related industries dominate billionaire wealth.
- Male billionaires represent the majority of total net worth, indicating a significant gender imbalance.
- The average age of billionaires highlights long term wealth accumulation trends.

## Business Implications
- Wealth concentration insights support economic analysis and market research.
- Industry level patterns can inform investment and sector performance studies.
- Demographic insights provide context for discussions around access to wealth creation.
- The dashboard enables consistent, high-level monitoring of global wealth distribution patterns.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates the ability to transform raw data into structured KPIs and business focused insights using Excel. It highlights how descriptive analytics and clear visualisation can support interpretation, discussion, and decision-making.
